  • expand_moreConfiguring S.M.A.R.T


    Configuring your room setup is the first step towards making your home smart. You can configure your system by selecting "Configure" build option from the "menu" menu located at the top right corner. You can customize room names. To customize, first enable the room by clicking on the checkbox at right side of the textbox. This will enable the room and now you will be able rename your room. After setting all rooms, click on the apply button to save your configuration.

  • expand_moreUpdating S.M.A.R.T


    We provide updates on regular basis to add new features to S.M.A.R.T to make your home smarter. Update option can be found inside the "menu" menu located at the top right corner. Click on the "check for updates" button system_update . If the updates are available, it will automatically download the updates. Keep in mind that you will need to restart/refresh your system to enable new features.

  • expand_moreApp is working but devices not responding.


    It may occur due to internal software errors & mostly occurs if your system is online continously for weeks without refreshing. In this case, you need to restart the system. Restart option is located in the menu of control page as shown in the above image. Restarting system will take few minutes.
