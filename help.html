  • expand_moreConfiguring S.M.A.R.T


    Configuring your room setup is the first step towards making your home smart. You can configure your system by selecting "Configure" build option from the "menu" menu located at the top right corner. You can customize room names. To customize, first enable the room by clicking on the checkbox at right side of the textbox. This will enable the room and now you will be able to rename your room. After setting all rooms, click on the apply button to save your configuration.

  • expand_moreUpdating S.M.A.R.T


    We provide updates on regular basis to add new features to S.M.A.R.T to make your home smarter. Update option can be found inside the "menu" menu located at the top right corner. Click on the "check for updates" button system_update . If the updates are available, it will automatically download the updates. Keep in mind that you will need to restart/refresh your system to enable new features.

  • expand_moreDevices not responding.


    It may occur due to internal software errors & mostly occurs if your system is online continously for weeks without refreshing. In this case, you need to restart the system. Restart autorenew option is located in the menu of control page as shown in the above image. Restarting system will take few minutes.

  • expand_moreSetting up profiles


    Profile simplifies task of switching devices by forming customizable & configurable groups of devices in each room. You can setup 3 separate profiles for each room. Press "Menu" more_vert button & go to the configuration page to setup/edit profiles. Then click on "profiles" tab. Select appropriate Room & Profile. You can edit profile name & configure which devices should be turned on/off for desired profile.

  • expand_moreApplying profile


    To apply previously configured profile, Click on "Person pin" icon person_pin (left to the menu button). It will display list of configured profiles. Apply the required profile by clicking on it. (If the profile isn't configured, it will be disabled & displayed in gray colour).

  • expand_moreTimer setup


    Timer can be used to create schedule for switching your devices on & off at particular time instances. To setup timer, click on the "clock" access_time button provided in front of switch. This will open a new popup. Provide turn on & turn off timings. Select daily switch if you want to switch device according to schedule everyday & Press Apply button. Devices with Red timer button indicates that timers are enabled for these devices. To disable the timer, click on the "Disable" button which is on the left side of "Apply" button in timer popup.
